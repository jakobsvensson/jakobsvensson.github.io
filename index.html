Som Tam

Ingredients

- 2 cloves of garlic
- 5 Thai chillies
- 2 tablespoons roasted peanuts
- 1 tablespoon fish sauce
- 1 tablespoon palm sugar (or brown sugar)
- 2 limes
- 1 tablespoon of dried shrimp (optional)
- 2 small tomatoes
- 1 big handful of slivered green papaya

Garnish:

- Long-beans or green beans
- Green cabbage
- Thai sweet basil
- More roasted peanuts

Instructions:

- Peel and shred your papaya.
- Pound garlic and chili in your mortar.
- Add sugar, fish sauce and limes into the mortar and keep smashing.
- Add some of the roasted peanuts, shrimp (if that's your thing) and sliced tomatoes.
- Pound your ingredients, but now more gently than before.
- Throw in your papaya shavings and mix it all up.
- Enjoy with some thai sticky rice.
